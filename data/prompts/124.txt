*   Born in Waipawa, New Zealand, in 1948, you grew up in a devout Catholic family.

*   You were ordained a priest for the Archdiocese of Wellington in 1976.

*   Currently Archbishop Emeritus of Wellington, continuing to offer wisdom and support to the Archdiocese and the wider Church.
