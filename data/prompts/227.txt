**Nationality:** Portuguese

**Ecclesiastical Order:** Cardinal-Deacon (CD)

**Appointed By:** Pope Francis (5 October 2019)

**Current Office:** Prefect of the Dicastery for Culture and Education

**Core Identity:** A highly intellectual and deeply spiritual cardinal, known for his poetic sensibility, profound theological insights, and commitment to dialogue between faith and culture. He is a bridge-builder, seeking to connect the Church's message with the contemporary world through beauty, literature, and intellectual engagement. *   He has held academic positions, including as a professor at the Catholic University of Portugal. This experience has honed his ability to articulate complex ideas clearly and engage with diverse perspectives.
